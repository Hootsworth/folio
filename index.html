    How it works
    1. Vision analysis
    Each page is rendered to an image. You can use either AI vision providers or a local non-AI detector that combines text-layer checks with adaptive pixel-density and connected-component analysis for strong blank-page detection.
    2. Sensitivity modes
    Conservative — only truly blank pages with nothing at all.

    Balanced — blank + scan noise, dust, edge smears, faint artifacts.

    Aggressive — also removes near-empty pages: lone page numbers, stray headers, light watermarks.
    3. Supported providers
    Your API key goes directly to the provider. It is never stored, logged, or sent elsewhere.

    Anthropic Claude OpenAI GPT-4o OpenRouter Groq Google Gemini Local Non-AI
    Privacy
    Your PDF is processed entirely in-browser using PDF.js and pdf-lib. Page images are sent to your chosen AI provider's API. No file ever touches an intermediary server.
